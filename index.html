I can be reached via Twitter, LinkedIn and email. I live in Toronto, Canada. My pronouns are he/him.

Who is this guy

I'm a policy advisor and data scientist. As a policy advisor, I provide advice to public institutions on how to respond to technology's influence on society, the economy and the state. As a data scientist, I analyze datasets for trends and predictions, and also (crucially) the limitations of experimental designs.

Work

I'm employed by City of Toronto Information and Technology, where I work on special projects for their executive team. Prior to this, I helped start the first Digital Government initiative in Canada, which became the Ontario Digital Service, and cofounded their policy team.

Other things

  • I co-founded Civic Tech Toronto. If you're in Toronto, consider attending a civic hacknight. I used to organize with Open Data Ottawa.
  • I have a Bachelors of Science in Mathematics.
